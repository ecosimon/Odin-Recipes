Description

Simple yet delicious steak recipe will have your guests drooling on dinner night.

Ingredients

  • 12oz steak
  • Butter and olive oil
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up a frying pan to medium-high heat
  2. Add in about 1-2 tblspoon of Olive oil
  3. When the oil is near smoking, gently add in the steak
  4. let the steak sit for 1-4 minutes depending how you like your steak
  5. Season your steak with salt and pepper and add in your butter, garlic and onions to add flavor
  6. After the amount of time to your liking has passed flip the steak over to the other side
  7. Plate and let it rest before cutting
  8. Enjoy!
