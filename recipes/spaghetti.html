Description

Spaghetti and Meatballs a classic and timeless dish over the centuries.

Following these simple steps you can make this amazing recipe for tonights dinner!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic and Onions
  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water and add in your pasta
  2. Set aside a frying pan to medium heat
  3. Toss in the garlic and onions until brown
  4. Add in the ground beef until thoroughly cooked
  5. Mix in your tomato sauce with the ground beef, onions and garlic
  6. When the Pasta is about Al dente, it's time to mix in your Pasta with the Ground beef sauce
  7. Enjoy!
