Description

Hamburgers! a staple dish in every household following this easy and simple recipe will have all your guests drooling tonight!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Shape your ground beef into patties
  2. Bring your frying pan to a medium/high heat
  3. Add in some olive oil and place patties into pan
  4. Let your patties sit in the pan depending to your liking
  5. prepare the onions and lettuce and buns on the side
  6. After flipping your burgers on the pan place your bun in the pan to sear them
  7. remove your buns and plate them with lettuce and onions and your condiment of choosing
  8. Voila enjoy!
